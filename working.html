How Skin Diagnosis Works

Step 1: Capture a clear image of the affected skin area using your device camera.
Step 2: Provide symptoms using voice input in your preferred language.
Step 3: The AI model analyzes the image and symptom data to detect possible skin conditions.
Step 4: Receive detailed results along with recommended actions and guidance.
Step 5: Save or share the report securely, if needed, for further consultation.
